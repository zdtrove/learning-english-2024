PERSONALITIES - LEADERSHIP

🎧 Do you want to be a leader?

No, I don't think I'd like to be a leader because I don't really have the qualities needed for that role. For example, my communication skills aren't particularly strong, so I might struggle to clearly explain ideas or motivate other people. On top of that, leaders usually have to deal with a lot of responsibility and pressure, and I'm not sure I'd enjoy being in that position. I'd rather focus on doing my own work well than managing a whole team

🎧 If you were a leader, and your teammates didn't respect you, what would you do?

If I were a leader and my teammates didn't respect me, I'd first try to understand the reasons behind it rather than getting upset. I would listen carefully to their opinions and ask for honest feedback about what I could do better. I believe respect is something that has to be earned, so I'd work on becoming more responsible, supportive, and fair when making decisions. Over time, I think showing good leadership through my actions would help me gain their trust and respect

🎧 What are the main qualities of being a good leader?

I think a good leader needs to have strong knowledge and experience in their field. When problems come up, they should be able to stay calm, think of effective solutions, and make good decisions instead of panicking. Another important quality is good communication. A leader should be able to explain the team's goals clearly, listen to other people's opinions, and make sure everyone is working toward the same objective. Finally, I think trust is essential. A good leader should keep their promises, take responsibility for their decisions, and set a good example for the rest of the team. When team members trust and respect their leader, they're usually more motivated to do their best.

🎧 Why do societies need leaders?

I think every society, organization, or team needs leaders because they provide direction and help people work toward a common goal. A good leader sets a clear vision, makes important decisions, and assigns tasks based on each person's strengths. They also help solve problems, keep everyone motivated, and make sure the team stays on track. Without someone to guide the group, it would be much harder to stay organized and achieve long-term goals.

🎧 Do you think a leader should be perfect in all fields?

No, I don't think that's necessary. No one can be an expert in every field, and leaders are no exception. I believe a good leader should have strong knowledge and experience in the areas related to their work, but they should also be willing to listen to others and keep learning. In fact, being open-minded and continuously improving themselves is often more important than trying to know everything

🎧 What are the responsibilities of a leader?

I think a leader's main responsibility is to guide the team towards achieving a common goal. They also need to assign tasks based on each member's strengths and solve any problems that come up during the project. Besides that, a good leader should motivate the team and make sure everyone works well together.

PERSONALITIES - HELP OTHERS

🎧 Do you like helping others?

Yes, I do. I've always been willing to help others whenever I can. For example, I sometimes take part in volunteer activities organized by non-profit organizations to support underprivileged children in rural or remote areas. Although I can't make a huge difference on my own, I believe that every small contribution can have a positive impact

🎧 When was your last time you helped others?

The last time I helped others was about a year ago, shortly after I graduated from university. My friends and I joined a volunteer trip to Sapa, where we taught English to children from underprivileged backgrounds. We also helped build a small library for the local community. It was a meaningful experience because we knew our efforts could make a positive difference in the children's education

🎧 Did your parents teach you the importance of helping others?

Yes, definitely. My parents have always taught me the importance of helping others, especially people who are less fortunate and elderly people. More importantly, they lead by example. Whenever our relatives, neighbors, or friends are in need, they're always willing to offer help. Growing up in that environment naturally encouraged me to do the same.

🎧 Have you ever refused to help others?

Yes, I have. I don't think helping others means saying yes to every request. For example, when I was at university, one of my classmates asked if they could copy my answers during an exam, but I refused. I didn't want to encourage dishonest behavior. I believe it's important to help people in the right way, rather than helping them do something that's unfair or against the rules.

🎧 Would you like to keep helping others in future?

Yes, definitely. I'd like to continue helping others whenever I have the opportunity, especially underprivileged children in rural or remote areas. I find volunteer work very meaningful because it gives me a sense of purpose and allows me to make a positive difference in other people's lives, even if it's only in a small way.

🎧 Do people nowadays help others more than in the past?

I don't think it's easy to compare people nowadays with those in the past. In every generation, there have always been kind and generous people who are willing to help others. However, I do think people today are a bit more cautious because they're more aware of scams and other social problems. As a result, they may hesitate before helping someone they don't know. That said, there are still plenty of people who volunteer, donate to charity, or support those in need whenever they can

🎧 Who should teach children to help others? Parents or teachers?

I think both parents and teachers play an equally important role. Parents are usually a child's first role models, so they can teach kindness and generosity through their own actions. At the same time, schools can reinforce these values by organizing volunteer activities or encouraging students to work together and help those in need. When children receive the same message at home and at school, they're more likely to develop these habits naturally.

🎧 In what kinds of professions do people help others more?

I think people in healthcare, education, emergency services, and non-profit organizations help others the most. For example, doctors, nurses, firefighters, and teachers all make a positive difference through their daily work. People who work for charities or non-profit organizations also dedicate a lot of their time to supporting underprivileged communities. Although these professions are different, they all share the same goal of improving other people's lives.

🎧 Why are some people willing to help others?

I think the main reason is empathy. People understand that anyone can go through difficult times, so they're more willing to offer help when someone is struggling. They know that one day they might be in the same situation themselves. Besides that, helping others often gives people a sense of satisfaction and purpose. Seeing that your support has made someone's life a little easier can be a very rewarding experience.
