PERSONALITIES - LEADERSHIP

🎧 Do you want to be a leader?

No, I don’t think I’d like to be a leader because I don’t really have the qualities needed for that role. For example, my communication skills aren’t particularly strong, so I might struggle to clearly explain ideas or motivate other people. On top of that, leaders usually have to deal with a lot of responsibility and pressure, and I’m not sure I’d enjoy being in that position. I’d rather focus on doing my own work well than managing a whole team

🎧 If you were a leader, and your teammates didn’t respect you, what would you do?

If I were a leader and my teammates didn’t respect me, I’d first try to understand the reasons behind it rather than getting upset. I would listen carefully to their opinions and ask for honest feedback about what I could do better. I believe respect is something that has to be earned, so I’d work on becoming more responsible, supportive, and fair when making decisions. Over time, I think showing good leadership through my actions would help me gain their trust and respect
