Yes, I think so. I'd like to make walking a regular part of my daily routine in the near future. It's something I've been meaning to do for quite a while, but I haven't been consistent with it yet. Not as much as they used to. Nowadays, most people prefer using motorcycles or other forms of transportation because they're convenient and relatively affordable. In a country like Vietnam, motorcycles are the most common way to get around, so people often choose to ride rather than walk. That said, some people, especially older adults and those who are health-conscious, still go for regular walks in parks or around their neighborhoods. 🎧 Do you think walking is important?

Yes, absolutely. I think walking is one of the easiest and most accessible forms of exercise. Even a short walk every day can help people stay fit and maintain a healthy lifestyle. It's also a great way to relax and clear your mind after a busy or stressful day at work. Another advantage is that walking is completely free, unlike going to the gym or joining a fitness club. All you need is a safe place to walk, such as a nearby park, and you can even enjoy it with friends or family

🎧 Do you think people will walk more or not in the future?

It's hard to say. On the one hand, people are becoming increasingly dependent on technology and tend to spend a lot of time sitting in front of screens, which may lead to a less active lifestyle. On the other hand, there's growing awareness of the importance of health and fitness these days. More people are using smartwatches and fitness apps to track their daily activity, including the number of steps they take. I think these trends could encourage people to walk more in the future. So, while I'm not completely sure, I'm cautiously optimistic that walking will become more popular

🎧 Do you think walking in the countryside is better than walking in the city?

Yes, definitely. Personally, I think walking in the countryside is much more enjoyable than walking in the city. One of the main reasons is that the air is usually much cleaner and fresher, which makes the experience more relaxing and healthier. In contrast, big cities often suffer from air pollution, noise, and heavy traffic. Another advantage of the countryside is the peaceful environment. You can enjoy nature, open spaces, and a slower pace of life, whereas walking in the city can sometimes be stressful because of traffic congestion and crowded streets

🎧 What could be done to improve the experience of walking in cities?

I think there are several things that could be done. First, people should be encouraged to walk more by raising awareness about the health benefits of walking and the risks associated with a sedentary lifestyle. However, encouraging people to walk is not enough. The government also needs to make cities more pedestrian-friendly by building wider sidewalks and improving road safety. In addition, creating more parks, green spaces, and walking paths would give residents more places to walk and relax. If cities become safer and more pleasant for pedestrians, I believe more people will choose walking as part of their daily routine

SPORTS - WATER SPORTS

🎧 Are water sports popular in your country?

Not really. Although Vietnam has a long coastline and plenty of rivers and beaches, water sports are not as popular as other outdoor activities. One reason is that water sports facilities and training centers are not widely available in many areas. In addition, some water sports can be quite expensive, which may discourage people from taking them up. That said, activities like swimming, kayaking, and jet skiing have become more popular in tourist destinations in recent years

🎧 What water sport is popular in Vietnam?

I'd say swimming is by far the most popular water sport in Vietnam. It's a basic life skill as well as a recreational activity, so many people learn it from a young age. In recent years, other water sports such as snorkeling, kayaking, and kitesurfing have also become available in some coastal tourist destinations. However, these activities are still relatively expensive and not easily accessible to everyone, so they haven't become as popular as swimming

🎧 What water sport do you often play?

If swimming is considered a water sport, then I'd say swimming is the only one I do regularly. I grew up in the countryside, where there were lots of rivers and rice fields nearby. When I was a child, my friends and I often went swimming in the river during the summer. It was a fun and refreshing way to spend time outdoors. Even now, I still enjoy swimming whenever I have the chance because it's both relaxing and good for my health

🎧 Do you want to play on a beach or near a lake?

I'd definitely choose a beach. There are usually more activities to enjoy there, such as swimming, beach volleyball, or simply relaxing by the sea. I also love the atmosphere of the beach, especially the fresh sea breeze and beautiful views. On a beach vacation, I can swim in the ocean, sunbathe, and enjoy fresh seafood, all in one place. In my opinion, beaches tend to be more lively and exciting than lakes, which is why I find them more appealing

🎧 Do you think water sports are dangerous to play?

Not necessarily. I think water sports can be quite safe as long as people follow the proper safety guidelines and have the necessary skills. For example, knowing how to swim and wearing appropriate safety equipment can significantly reduce the risks. Of course, some water sports are more challenging than others, but with proper training and supervision, I don't think they are overly dangerous

🎧 When you were young, did you like playing with water?

Yes, absolutely. I grew up in the countryside, so I spent a lot of time outdoors with my friends. Back then, we didn't have smartphones or other electronic devices to keep us entertained, so we played traditional games like hide-and-seek, football, and many other outdoor activities. We also loved swimming and fishing in the nearby river. During the summer, we would spend hours in the water, and sometimes it felt like we were at the river more often than at home

🎧 What are the advantages of water sports?

I think water sports offer a wide range of benefits. First of all, they're a great way to stay fit because they involve physical activity and help improve overall fitness. They can also help reduce stress and improve mental well-being, as many people find being in or around water very relaxing. Another advantage is that water sports are often done in groups, which gives people the opportunity to meet new friends and develop better social skills. Overall, they're both enjoyable and beneficial for physical and mental health

🎧 Why do people have a good time when they are near water?

I think it's because being near water helps people feel relaxed and refreshed. Whether it's the sea, a lake, or a river, the peaceful atmosphere can help people forget about their daily worries and reduce stress. There are also plenty of enjoyable activities to do, such as swimming, boating, or simply sitting by the water and enjoying the view. On a hot day, being near water is especially pleasant because it helps people cool down and feel more comfortable

🎧 What are some of the key benefits and drawbacks of water transport?

I think water transport has both advantages and disadvantages. One major advantage is that ships can carry a huge amount of cargo and large numbers of passengers, making them very efficient for long-distance transportation. Another benefit is that waterways such as rivers, seas, and oceans already exist, so there's less need to build expensive infrastructure compared to roads or railways. However, there are some drawbacks as well. The biggest one is probably speed. Water transport is generally much slower than air travel, especially for international journeys. In addition, accidents such as oil spills can have a serious impact on the environment and harm marine ecosystems. So, while water transport is cost-effective and efficient in many cases, it also comes with certain limitations

🎧 Was transport by water more important in the past than it is nowadays?

Yes, I believe it was. In the past, water transport played a much more important role because there were fewer transportation options available. Before the development of modern infrastructure such as highways, railways, and airports, rivers and seas were often the most efficient way to move people and goods over long distances. Nowadays, people have access to many faster and more convenient forms of transport, especially air travel and road transportation. As a result, water transport is still important for shipping cargo, but it's no longer the primary means of transportation for most people

🎧 Why has water become an important resource?

Water is one of the most important resources on Earth because all living things depend on it to survive. Humans, animals, and plants all need water for their daily activities and basic biological processes. It's essential for drinking, growing food, maintaining hygiene, and supporting ecosystems. In fact, when scientists search for signs of life on other planets, one of the first things they look for is the presence of water. Without it, life as we know it would not be possible
