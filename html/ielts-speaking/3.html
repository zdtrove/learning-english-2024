SHOPPING

🎧 Do you enjoy shopping?

Yes, I do. Although I'm not really a shopaholic, I genuinely enjoy shopping from time to time. I like wandering around shopping malls, browsing different stores, and doing a bit of window shopping without necessarily buying anything. There's something relaxing about looking at new products and exploring what's available. I especially enjoy visiting grocery stores because the clean environment and the fresh produce always give me a pleasant feeling and make the experience more enjoyable

🎧 How often do you go shopping?

I'd say I go shopping about once or twice a month. Most of the time, I don't really plan my shopping trips in advance. Sometimes, I buy things on impulse, especially when I come across something that catches my attention. I'm not the type of person who closely follows the latest fashion trends, but if I see an item that I really like, I tend to buy it right away without overthinking it

🎧 Where do you usually shop?

I don't usually shop at just one place. I have a few favorite stores because each of them offers a different style and selection of clothing. From time to time, I also like visiting high-end fashion stores and trying on designer brands. Like many people, I enjoy treating myself occasionally, so if I've been working hard or achieved something important, I might splurge on a quality item as a reward for my efforts

🎧 What is the most expensive item that you have purchased?

Although I enjoy shopping, the most expensive item I've ever bought for myself is a watch that I purchased a couple of months ago. I'm a big fan of the brand, so when I saw it being advertised at a discount, I became interested right away. Even though it was still quite expensive and cost me a significant amount of money, I decided to buy it because I really liked its design. What I love most about it is its simple yet elegant style, and it's also very durable, so I think it was worth the investment

🎧 Do you often shop online?

Yes, I do. In fact, I shop online quite regularly because it's extremely convenient and time-saving. I often order lunch through food delivery apps and buy everyday items such as pens, notebooks, or other small necessities from online shopping platforms. Since everything can be done with just a few taps on my phone, online shopping helps me save a lot of time and effort, especially on busy workdays

🎧 What are the advantages and disadvantages of online shopping?

Online shopping has both advantages and disadvantages. One of the biggest benefits is convenience. People can buy almost anything they need without having to travel to physical stores, which saves a lot of time and effort. Another advantage is that it's easy to compare prices and read customer reviews, allowing shoppers to find better deals and make more informed decisions. In addition, since people can take their time before placing an order, online shopping may help reduce impulsive purchases. However, there are also some drawbacks. For example, the actual products may not always match the photos or descriptions shown online, which can lead to disappointment. Another disadvantage is that customers cannot see, touch, or try products before buying them, especially clothing or shoes. As a result, there is always a risk that the item may not meet their expectations

🎧 Do you think it is safe to shop online?

Not entirely. Although online shopping is very convenient, I think there are still some risks involved. For example, if people buy products from unreliable websites or sellers, they may end up receiving counterfeit or low-quality goods. This can be particularly concerning when it comes to food, cosmetics, or health-related products. Another major issue is online security. Some shoppers may become victims of scams or have their personal and financial information stolen by hackers if they are not careful when making online purchases. Therefore, I think online shopping can be safe, but only if people use trusted platforms and take proper precautions

🎧 Do you think the government should impose more control on online shopping?

No, I don't think the government should place too many restrictions on online shopping. Although there are some risks associated with it, online shopping offers a lot of benefits to both consumers and businesses. For example, online sellers often have lower operating costs because they don't need to maintain large physical stores. As a result, they can sometimes offer products at more competitive prices. If the government introduced excessive regulations or additional taxes, it could increase costs for sellers, which would likely lead to higher prices for consumers. Therefore, I think the government should focus on protecting consumers from fraud and unsafe products rather than limiting online shopping itself

🎧 Why do some people like shopping with friends?

I think many people enjoy shopping with friends because it makes the experience more enjoyable and practical. For one thing, friends can give honest opinions and helpful advice, especially when you're unsure whether a particular item suits you or is worth buying. They may also have a good sense of fashion and help you make better choices. In addition, shopping with friends can help you avoid overspending because they can remind you to stick to your budget. On top of that, shopping becomes a social activity, allowing people to spend quality time together while exploring different stores

WEATHER / SEASONS

🎧 What is the weather like in your country?

Vietnam has a tropical climate, so the weather is generally warm throughout the year. However, it varies from region to region. In the north, there are four distinct seasons: spring, summer, autumn, and winter. Summers can be extremely hot and humid, while winters are sometimes surprisingly cold. In contrast, the south mainly experiences two seasons: the rainy season and the dry season. Overall, the weather is quite pleasant, although it can occasionally be unpredictable. Snow is extremely rare and is only seen in a few mountainous areas during particularly cold winters

🎧 Which months have the best weather in your country?

I think it really depends on personal preferences, but for me, the best weather is usually in autumn, especially around September to November. During this time, the temperatures are generally mild and comfortable, and the weather is neither too hot nor too cold. I also enjoy autumn because there are fewer extreme weather conditions, such as heatwaves or heavy storms, which can sometimes affect my mood and daily activities. Overall, I find it the most pleasant and relaxing season of the year

🎧 Does it bother you much when it rains?

It depends on the situation. If I'm on my way to work, meeting friends, or doing something outdoors and suddenly get caught in the rain, it can be quite annoying, especially if I end up completely soaked. However, during a hot spell, I actually enjoy the rain because it cools the air down and makes the weather much more comfortable. In that sense, rain can be both inconvenient and refreshing, depending on the circumstances

🎧 Does the weather influence your mood?

Yes, it definitely does. My mood is often affected by the weather. For example, when it rains continuously for several days, I tend to feel a bit gloomy because I can't go out and spend time with my friends as much as I'd like. On the other hand, when the weather is extremely hot and humid, I usually feel tired and less energetic. In contrast, cool and sunny days tend to put me in a better mood and make me feel more motivated to do outdoor activities

🎧 Is it worse to feel too hot or too cold?

I think it's worse to feel too hot. When it's cold, you can always put on more clothes, use a blanket, or turn on a heater to keep warm. However, when the weather is extremely hot and humid, it can be difficult to stay comfortable, even with fans or air conditioners. The heat often makes me feel exhausted and unmotivated, and I find it hard to concentrate on my work. It becomes even more unbearable during a power outage because there's no way to cool down. That's why I'd much rather be too cold than too hot

🎧 Do you always pay attention to the weather forecast?

Yes, I do, but not all the time. I usually check the weather forecast whenever I have outdoor plans or important events coming up. Since the weather in Vietnam can be quite unpredictable, knowing the forecast helps me prepare in advance and avoid disruptions caused by rain or extreme weather conditions. However, I don't spend too much time watching weather reports. If I'm busy, I simply check a weather app on my phone, which only takes a few seconds and gives me all the information I need

🎧 Does air pollution affect the weather?

Yes, I think air pollution can affect the weather to some extent. In many large cities, high levels of air pollution can lead to smog, which makes the sky look hazy and reduces visibility. As a result, the weather may appear gloomy and unpleasant, even on days when it isn't actually raining. Moreover, poor air quality can make people feel uncomfortable and negatively affect their mood. That's why air pollution is becoming a growing concern in many urban areas

🎧 Do you think that weather affects people's behaviour?

Yes, I believe weather can have a significant impact on people's behaviour and mood. For example, when the weather is extremely hot and humid, people tend to become more irritable, impatient, and less energetic. On the other hand, when the weather is cool and breezy, people usually feel more relaxed, comfortable, and positive. As a result, they may be more willing to socialize, spend time outdoors, or simply enjoy their daily activities. So, in my opinion, weather can definitely influence how people feel and behave

🎧 How do the different seasons affect the lifestyle of people in your country?

I think the different seasons influence people's lifestyles in several ways, especially their clothing choices and leisure activities. For example, during the hot summer months, people tend to wear light and comfortable clothes such as T-shirts, shorts, and breathable fabrics to stay cool. In contrast, during colder periods, especially in northern Vietnam, many people prefer wearing jackets, sweaters, or coats to keep warm. The seasons also affect how people spend their free time. In summer, many people enjoy going to the beach, swimming, or taking part in other outdoor activities. On the other hand, spring is often considered a great time for picnics and sightseeing because the weather is mild and pleasant. Overall, I think seasonal changes have a noticeable impact on people's daily routines and recreational preferences

🎧 Do you think there are any problems with the world's climate now?

Yes, definitely. I think climate change has become one of the biggest global challenges today. In many countries, including Vietnam, people are experiencing more frequent heatwaves, longer periods of extreme weather, and increasingly severe floods and storms. These natural disasters can cause significant damage to infrastructure, homes, and agriculture, and unfortunately, they sometimes result in injuries and loss of life. Another major concern is the melting of polar ice caps and glaciers, which contributes to rising sea levels. This poses a serious threat to coastal areas and low-lying regions around the world. If these issues are not addressed effectively, they could have long-term consequences for both the environment and human society

🎧 Do you think fighting global climate change is a governmental responsibility or the responsibility of individuals?

I think both governments and individuals share the responsibility for tackling climate change. On the one hand, governments play a crucial role in introducing environmental policies, investing in renewable energy, and launching public awareness campaigns. These measures can encourage people and businesses to adopt more sustainable practices. On the other hand, individuals also have an important part to play. People can make small but meaningful changes in their daily lives, such as reducing waste, recycling, conserving energy, and using public transport whenever possible. In my opinion, climate change is such a large-scale issue that it can only be addressed effectively if both governments and citizens work together

CITY / COUNTRYSIDE

🎧 Do you often visit the countryside?

Yes, I do. I usually visit the countryside about once a month to get away from the hustle and bustle of city life. I really enjoy being surrounded by nature and breathing in the fresh air. It helps me relax, clear my mind, and recharge after a busy and stressful month at work

🎧 If so, who do you visit in the countryside?

Well, it depends. If I just want some peace and quiet, I usually go to the countryside by myself to relax and think about my future plans. On the other hand, if I feel like spending quality time with my friends, I'll invite them to join me. We can enjoy the scenery, have some good conversations, and take a break from our busy routines together

🎧 Would you like to live in the countryside in the future?

Yes, definitely. I'm a big nature lover, so I've always liked the idea of settling down in the countryside someday. Life there is generally much more peaceful, the air is cleaner, and the cost of living is usually lower than in big cities. I think it would be a great place to enjoy a slower and more relaxing lifestyle

🎧 Have areas of the countryside in your country changed since you were a child?

Yes, they've changed quite a lot since I was a child. One of the biggest reasons is industrialization. Many green fields and open spaces have gradually been replaced by factories, industrial zones, and other developments. Another noticeable change is that a lot of young people move to big cities for education and job opportunities, so many rural communities are now mainly made up of older people and children

🎧 Do you think that visits to the countryside should be something that schools offer to their children ?

Yes, absolutely. I think schools should organize more trips to the countryside for children. These visits give them a chance to learn about nature, animals, and rural life firsthand rather than spending all their time in front of screens. They also encourage children to take part in outdoor activities, which can improve their physical health and help them develop a more active lifestyle

🎧 What are the advantages of living in the countryside?

There are quite a few advantages to living in the countryside. First of all, it can be beneficial for people's health because the air is usually cleaner and less polluted than in big cities. As a result, residents may be less likely to suffer from respiratory problems and other health issues related to air pollution. Another major advantage is the lower cost of living. Housing, food, and other daily expenses are often more affordable, which can reduce financial pressure and help people enjoy a more comfortable lifestyle

🎧 What are the disadvantages of living in the countryside?

Although living in the countryside has many benefits, it also comes with some drawbacks. One major disadvantage is the lack of job opportunities. Compared to big cities, there are fewer career options available, which is why many young people choose to move to urban areas for work. Another downside is the limited entertainment and recreational facilities. For example, it can be difficult to find shopping malls, cinemas, or other leisure venues in rural areas. In addition, some remote villages still lack basic infrastructure and services, such as access to clean water and high-quality healthcare

🎧 What kinds of people live in the countryside?

I think different types of people choose to live in the countryside. One group is older people or retirees who prefer a quieter and less stressful lifestyle. After spending many years working in busy cities, they often want to slow down and enjoy a more peaceful environment. Another group is nature lovers. They enjoy being surrounded by greenery, breathing fresh air, and living closer to nature. Some people also choose the countryside because they value a slower pace of life and a stronger sense of community

🎧 How has life changed over time in the countryside?

Life in the countryside has changed significantly over the past few decades. One of the biggest changes is in agriculture. Thanks to modern technology and machinery, farmers can now work more efficiently and produce larger amounts of food with less physical effort than before. Another noticeable change is the migration of young people to cities. Many leave rural areas to pursue better education and job opportunities, which means that many villages are now populated mainly by older people and children

🎧 Do you think it is better to live in the countryside or in the city?

That's a difficult question because both the countryside and the city have their own advantages and disadvantages. However, if I had to choose, I'd probably prefer living in the countryside. The cleaner air and quieter environment can be much better for both physical and mental health. In addition, life in big cities is often fast-paced and stressful, whereas the countryside offers a more peaceful atmosphere where people can relax and escape from the hustle and bustle of urban life

PERSONALITIES - TIME MANAGEMENT

🎧 Is it important to be punctual?

Absolutely. I think punctuality is a very important quality in both personal and professional life. Being on time shows that you are responsible, reliable, and respectful of other people's time. It also helps create a positive impression and build trust. On the other hand, being consistently late can make others see you as careless or unprofessional

🎧 How do you feel when others are late?

I think it depends on the situation. If someone is late because they were careless, such as oversleeping or forgetting about an appointment, I can get quite annoyed because it shows a lack of respect for other people's time. However, if they have a valid reason, such as heavy traffic, bad weather, or a health issue, I'm usually quite understanding. After all, some situations are simply beyond a person's control

🎧 Are you, yourself, late very often?

No, not really. I'm usually quite punctual because I value both my own time and other people's time. I always try to plan ahead and arrive on time for appointments or meetings. That said, there are occasions when I'm late due to unexpected situations, such as heavy traffic or a health problem. In those cases, I usually let the other person know as soon as possible and apologize for the inconvenience

🎧 How often do you check the time?

I check the time quite frequently throughout the day. Because of my job, I need to manage my schedule carefully and make sure I stay on track with my tasks. I prefer planning my work in advance rather than rushing to finish everything at the last minute. Constantly racing against deadlines can be stressful and exhausting, so keeping an eye on the time helps me stay organized and productive

🎧 Do you wear a watch?

Yes, I do. I wear a watch almost every day because it helps me keep track of time more conveniently. Even though I can check the time on my phone, I find it much quicker and more practical to simply glance at my watch. Besides that, I think a watch is a stylish accessory that can complement an outfit and make a person look more professional and well put together

🎧 What do you do when you wait?

To be honest, waiting is not something I particularly enjoy. Whenever I have to wait, I usually find ways to keep myself occupied. For example, I might browse social media, reply to messages, make a phone call, or read a book. Doing these activities helps pass the time more quickly and makes waiting feel much less boring

🎧 Why are some people always late?

There are several reasons why some people are always late. One common reason is poor time management. They may underestimate how long it takes to get ready or travel to a place, which causes them to fall behind schedule. Another reason is procrastination. Some people tend to put things off until the last minute and then end up rushing. In addition, a few people simply don't see punctuality as a priority, so they aren't very concerned about arriving on time

🎧 How do you feel when people keep you waiting?

It really depends on the reason. If someone is late because of circumstances beyond their control, such as heavy traffic or an unexpected work commitment, I'm usually quite understanding. However, if they keep me waiting because they overslept or didn't manage their time properly, I can get quite frustrated. I believe that time is valuable, and making someone wait unnecessarily can be seen as disrespectful to their time and effort

🎧 How can people balance their time between work and private life?

I think maintaining a healthy work-life balance requires good time management. One effective way is to create a daily to-do list and prioritize the most important tasks. When people stay focused and avoid distractions, they can complete their work more efficiently and have more free time afterward. It’s also important to set clear boundaries between work and personal life. For example, limiting time spent on smartphones and social media can help people stay productive and make more time for family, hobbies, and relaxation
