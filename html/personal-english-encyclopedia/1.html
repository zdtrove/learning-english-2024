MY HOME

I currently live in Vision Apartment, which is located in Tan Tao Ward, Binh Tan District. I moved there about a year ago after buying my own apartment, and overall, I'm quite satisfied with the place.

The apartment complex was designed to be built in three phases. At the moment, only the first phase has been completed, which consists of four buildings named A, B, C, and D. Two of them have 25 floors, while the other two have 19 floors. The remaining two phases, which will include another six buildings, are still at the foundation stage, so I think it will probably take quite a long time before the entire project is finished. In total, the complex will eventually have ten buildings.

I live on the ninth floor of Building A, one of the 25-story towers. Each building has two elevators, but unfortunately, I've noticed that they break down quite frequently since I moved in. Recently, many residents complained that the elevators became extremely hot, especially during peak hours, so the management installed additional fans to improve ventilation. It has made the elevators much more comfortable to use.

One thing I really like about my apartment complex is its spacious environment. It has wide internal roads, a beautiful park with plenty of benches and trees, and a large pond right in the middle of the park. I often take a walk there in the evening because it's peaceful and relaxing after a busy day at work.

Another advantage is its convenient location. It only takes about seven minutes to get to GO An Lac Supermarket and around ten minutes to reach AEON Mall Binh Tan, so shopping or having a meal is very convenient.

The surrounding infrastructure has also improved a lot recently. In the past, the road leading to the apartment complex was often flooded whenever there were unusually high tides. However, after both sides of the Tham Luong Canal were reinforced, flooding has become much less of a problem. In addition, street vendors used to occupy part of the road, making traffic quite congested. Fortunately, the local authorities have cleared most of them away, so commuting has become much smoother and more convenient.
